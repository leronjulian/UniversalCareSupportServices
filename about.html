At Universal Care Support Services:


Our Mission Statement:

- Universal Care Support Services LLC Mission is to provide outstanding services and to unlock potentials in each individual by empowering and linking them with all availabe and necessary resources and right services which will help them to be more productive citizens and meet their individualized and unique needs. We will advocate and communicate on our recipients behalf, we will give them opportunity to be exposed and experience what is important to them so that they can live a self-satisfying and rewarding life. We will connect them with resources and programs.


Goal:

- Universal Care Support Services primary goal is to link, advocate, monitor, and coordinate services which will benefit our individuals by helping them to be more active and positive members in society. We are going to seek solutions and resources to problems


Vision:

- Universal Care Support Services Vision is to enhance each individual by giving them an opportunity to achieve their goals and dreams and empowering them with resources which will enable them an opportunity to live a self sufficient and rewarding life.
